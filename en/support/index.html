One by One Support

Last updated: June 5, 2026

One by One is an iPhone and Apple Watch app that helps turn a stuck moment or short input into a small Begin flow users can start immediately. AI-backed Begin generation and step adjustment may use remote AI processing to provide app functionality.

Request Help

Use the project issue page to report problems or request support.

Open GitHub Issues

Do not include sensitive information such as personal schedules, payment receipts, Apple IDs, or phone numbers in public issues.

Helpful Details for Issue Reports

  • Device model and iOS or watchOS version
  • App version and build number
  • The screen where the issue occurred
  • What you tried to do and what actually happened
  • Whether the issue involves Apple Watch, Live Activity, StoreKit purchase, Calendar export, location, remote AI, or notifications

Purchases and Subscriptions

Apple handles One by One Pro purchases, subscription renewals, cancellations, and refund requests through the user's App Store account. Subscriptions can be managed from iOS Settings or the App Store account subscription screen.

Privacy

Device storage, iCloud sync, and remote AI processing are described in the Privacy Policy.
