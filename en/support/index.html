OneByOne Support

Last updated: May 29, 2026

OneByOne is an iPhone and Apple Watch app that helps turn a stuck state or a short input into a small Begin flow the user can run immediately. AI-powered Begin generation and step adjustment may use remote AI processing to provide app functionality.

Get help

Please use the project issue page to report a problem or request support.

Open GitHub Issues

Do not include sensitive information such as personal schedules, health information, purchase receipts, Apple ID, or phone numbers in public issues.

Useful details for reports

  • Device model and iOS or watchOS version
  • App version and build number
  • The screen where the issue occurred
  • What you were trying to do and what happened instead
  • Whether the issue relates to Apple Watch, Live Activity, StoreKit purchases, Calendar export, health records, location, remote AI, or notifications

Purchases and subscriptions

OneByOne Pro purchases, subscription renewal, cancellation, and refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store account. Subscriptions can be managed in iOS Settings or the App Store account subscription management screen.

Privacy

Device storage, iCloud sync, and remote AI processing are described in the Privacy Policy.
